## Introduction

While fireworks went up, shots rang out. News24 reports three taxi drivers killed across two New Year's Eve incidents in Mpumalanga. Route disputes are suspected; commuters and operators are tense.

## Main Content

### What Happened

- Two separate attacks, same night; investigations point to route or turf tensions.
- SAPS on-scene; no suspects named yet.
- Industry forums calling for mediated talks to prevent reprisals.

### Why It Matters

- Taxi routes are economic arteries; instability disrupts commuters and local commerce.
- Violence risks rerouting commuters into costlier or unsafe alternatives.
- Provincial authorities face pressure to enforce existing agreements and bolster patrols.
